I am passionate about economics, physics, mathematics and political science. My senior year I plan to take inroductory economics as well as policial sicence at the University of Chicago while also taking AT Physics II, Statistics, English 3/4 and AP Calculus BC.

Students Against Brain Cancer, a club that I founded with a friend, is a community service organization that participates in volunteer events as well as organizes fundraising events. More information can be found on the page I creatd for the club (linked above).
